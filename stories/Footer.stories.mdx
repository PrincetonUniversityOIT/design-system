# Footer

The "Footer" is a section at the bottom of the page that is generally repeated for each page in the application.

## Implementation Notes

The footer tag is used and assigned a role of contentinfo to "identify information repeated at the end of every page
of a website, including copyright information, navigation links, and privacy statements" (<a href="https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/Accessibility/ARIA/Roles/Contentinfo_role">ARIA: contentinfo role</a>).
